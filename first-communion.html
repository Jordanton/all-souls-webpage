HOLY COMMUNION

The Holy Eucharist is the second Sacrament of Initiation. It is associated with Baptism and Confirmation. Christ instituted the Holy Eucharist at the Last Supper; this Sacrament is of thanksgiving and praise. It is a Sacramental sacrifice that intensifies our union with Christ, separates us from sin, and builds the unity of the faithful.
