CONFIRMATION

Christ instituted the Sacrament of Confirmation on his apostles when he conferred with the Holy Spirit on Easter Sunday and Pentecost. Confirmation increases and deepens Baptismal grace and unites us more firmly to Christ. Confirmation gives us the strength to spread and defend the Catholic faith.

About the Program

Preparation for the Sacrament of Confirmation is a two-year program, offered in English and Spanish. Anyone from the ages of 14, or has begun 9th grade, until the age of 17 may attend.
