All Souls Catholic Church

RITE OF CHRISTIAN INITIATION FOR ADULTS

Are you curious about the Catholic Church?

Are you missing any of your Sacraments?

Then this is a place to have your questions answered. It’s a place to find out if the Catholic Church is where God is calling you. Whatever your religious background, we welcome and value it. The past for each of us is where we have been on our journey to God. We invite you to grow in your relationship with God.

Our purpose is to help you find out about the Catholic Church and, if it is appropriate, to assist in your discernment about becoming a member. If that is your decision, we are here to help in your preparation for the sacraments of initiation – Baptism, Confirmation and Eucharist (Holy Communion). After these sacraments we want to help you to be the best Catholic Christian you can be.

Mission Statement

The Rite of Christian Initiation of Adults is a rite by which those of catechetical age enter the Catholic Church. It is a faith journey that brings people together in community to renew heart, mind and spirit, guided by members of the parish who strive to live by the Gospel of Jesus. This is a personal journey to a deeper relationship with God tempered with the realization that each person’s gifts have been given to them to benefit the community. The enthusiasm of newcomers encountering Christ can have the effect of renewing the whole parish.
